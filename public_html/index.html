Ricks And Mortz

Ricks and Mortz is an American adult animated science fiction sitcom created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon for Cartoon Network's late-night programming block Adult Swim.

All seasons

Season one

The first season of the American animated television series, Ricks and Mortz originally aired in the United States on Cartoon Network's late night programming block, Adult Swim. It premiered on December 2, 2013 with "Pilot" and ended on April 14, 2014 with "Ricksy Business", with a total of eleven episodes. The first season received critical acclaim.

Summary

Ricks convinces his daughter, Beth, and his son-in-law, Jerry, to allow their son, Mortz, to continue following him in his interdimensional adventures, promising that this will improve his school performance. Mortz, his parents and his sister, Summer, are dragged into dangerous adventures throughout the multiverse, which they manage to escape mostly thanks to Ricks's ingenuity.

When Ricks and Mortz accidentally turn the population of Earth into monsters, they move to an alternate dimension where the disaster was averted, leaving Beth, Jerry and Summer behind. They adapt to a seemingly identical reality, as they take the place of alternate versions of themselves who died moments before their arrival. It is revealed that Ricks keeps using Mortz as his companion, because his inferior brain waves neutralize Ricks's genius brain waves, making him undetectable. When Beth and Jerry go on vacation, Ricks and the kids have a party with numerous guests that turn the house into a mess. Ricks freezes time so that he and the kids can repair the house, before their parents return.

Main Characters

Ricks Sanchez

Ricks Sanchez is the co-eponymous main character and leading protagonist of the show. He is a genius scientist whose alcoholism and reckless, nihilistic behavior are a source of concern for his daughter's family, as well as the safety of their son, Mortz. He is voiced by Justin Roiland.

Mortz Smith

Mortimer "Mortz" Smith Sr. is one of the two eponymous main protagonists in Ricks and Mortz. He is the grandson of Ricks and is often forced to tag along on his various misadventures. Mortz attends Harry Herpson High School along with his sister, Summer.

Jerry Smith

Jerry Smith is the husband of Beth Smith, the father of Summer Smith and Mortz Smith,he currently acts as the father and the son-in-law of the Mortz Smith and Ricks Sanchez from Dimension C-137, respectively.
